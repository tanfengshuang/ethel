This tool provides basic access to the Red Hat's stage customer accounts management. In other words it allows you to create customer account, populate it with subscriptions and activate it. All in staged environment, using Stage Candlepin and Stage Customer Portal.

Create Accounts

By feeding the tool with Username and Password you can easily create an account, which you can later use when logging into Stage Customer Portal or when activating a subscription on your machine.

As an option we're offering to you in the same step to populate the account with Subscription SKUs. These are identified by SKU ID (you can lookup all available SKUs to subscribe via Search SKU Catalog tab). If it is necessary you can specify Quantity as well as Expiration Date. You can always come back and add another Subscriptions for existing accounts in the Add Subscription tab.

And finally, when you're populating your account with Subscriptions, in order to use them, you have to accept Red Hat's Terms and Conditions. This can be done once you log in into Stage Customer Portal, or via our checkbox.

View Account

Listing your account's Subscriptions is essential functionality for Ethel. Give Ethel you credentials and she's gonna tell you what Subscriptions you have, what are their Subscription SKU ID, Pool ID, Quantity and specifically what Product Name does it refer to. For further details on a SKU you can always lookup the SKU Catalog.

Activate Account

Did you forgot to check the Accept Terms and Conditions checkbox? Here's a thing. You can go to Stage Customer Portal or use this tab and Ethel does it for you.

Import and Export

The Stage Candlepin is refreshed time to time. That means all accounts there are deleted. If you have built a relationship with your accounts, you can head to this tab and make a backup. And once you want them back, just upload your backup and it's done!

Add Subsctiption

By adding a Subscription you're creating a new Subscription Pool. Such pool can have couple parameters set via this tool. Of course, it is the SKU, you're attaching. In addition there's a Quantity and Expiration Date. Subscriptions we're offering are 1 year long, so expiration date can be at most 1 year from now. Shorter time means we create a pool that already started days before (so if you want a subscription to expire today, it actually started year ago).

Refresh Subscriptions

Refreshing is a process of forced initialization of you Subscriptions. Is there something wrong with your account? Head over here and hit Refresh!. It's the first aid when something is not working.

Search SKU Catalog

We're offering two basic approaches how to search the database. First, the simpler method is to use a Product Name. The other option is to specify the criteria yourself.

When you like the results of your search, you can download them! Just hit the Kebab menu on far right!
